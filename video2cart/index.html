How it works

  • Play the video, by click on play button.
  • Now play will trun to pause, Just pause the video where you want see cart object in current frame.
  • If any cart objects present in current frame, water mark object will appear top of the video.
  • Click top of those cart objects to go to shopping site.
  • Turn on trace, for finding cart objects while playing itself.

Note :- This is demo programe and it will work only in latest browsers currenlty and screen size 1024 x 768
